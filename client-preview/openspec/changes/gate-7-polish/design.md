# Gate 7 Polish Design

## Design Direction

Asc3nd should feel like a sharp youth-centered nonprofit campaign, not a generic nonprofit template. The visual system must stay grounded in the flyer: black, gold, white, bold condensed type, youth imagery, strong Donate path, and community trust.

## Gate Sequence

| Gate | Scope | Allowed files |
|---|---|---|
| 7C | Hero and nav polish | `index.html`, `styles.css`, `app.js`, assets if needed |

| 7J | Preview deployment | deploy config/docs only after human approval |

## Design Rules

- Keep the logo visible near the top-left title.
- Do not hide Donate behind clever UI.
- Do not bury Volunteer/Contact.
- Do not ship dead links.
- Every coming-soon action must open a clear modal with one sentence and one real next action.
- Light mode must be warm paper/black/gold, not bland white.
- Motion must respect reduced-motion and never delay Donate visibility.
- No fake testimonials, fake stats, fake partnerships, fake checkout, or fake backend claims.
